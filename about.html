    Game Mini Carnival

    What is GAMEINC?

    Game Mini Carnival or known as GAMEINC is an event that is annually held for showcasing games that MC111 students have produced throughout semesters 3 and 4 of their studies. This event has become one of our main events since 2016.

    Generally throughout GAMEINC, people can try our games and give feedback on video games created by MC111 students. There are many categories competed and the best participants from each category will receive their rewards based on their efforts over 2 semesters.

    This carnival takes place at UiTM Alor Gajah, Melaka from 2016-2019 but due to COVID-19 pandemic that invaded the world, this carnival had to be organized online from 2020.
